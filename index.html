Navigating Regulatory Friction in a Fragmented Digital World.

The Berlin Institute for Digital Strategy (BIDS) is an independent intelligence hub dedicated to auditing the intersection of technology, law, and geopolitics. We deliver evidence-based assessments on export controls, digital sovereignty, and cross-border regulatory risks to support strategic decision-making in an era of technological decoupling.

Institutional Capabilities

Strategic Auditing

Systematic monitoring of non-Western digital regulatory landscapes, utilizing custom NLP pipelines to identify emerging compliance risks and strategic pivots.

Geopolitical Intelligence

Mapping institutional alignments and technological dependencies to provide actionable risk assessments for cross-border digital operations.
