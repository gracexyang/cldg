Data-Driven Intelligence for the New Digital Order.

The Berlin Institute for Digital Strategy (BIDS) is an independent research hub at the intersection of International Relations and Computational Social Science. We provide evidence-based analysis on global cyber governance, digital sovereignty, and the evolving technological alignment between major powers.

Core Capabilities

Computational Auditing

Utilizing high-scale NLP pipelines to audit legislative shifts and discursive trends in multi-lingual policy corpora.

Network Intelligence

Applying relational analysis to map strategic dependencies and institutional alignments in global technology supply chains.
