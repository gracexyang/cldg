Independent Computational Research for Global Digital Governance.

The Berlin Institute for Digital Strategy (BIDS) is a research-led initiative dedicated to auditing digital norms and geopolitical friction. We bridge International Relations theory with Computational Social Science to provide evidence-based insights.

Institutional Methodology

Computational Policy Auditing

BIDS maintains proprietary Python-based pipelines for multi-lingual policy extraction, automating the detection of semantic shifts in official strategy documents.

Network Analysis (SNA)

Utilizing relational data modeling to map institutional interdependencies and the diffusion of digital norms within international organizations.
